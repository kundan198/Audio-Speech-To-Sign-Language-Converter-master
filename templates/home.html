The flagship mode
Healthcare Mode

Built for the doctor's office.

Deaf patients face 3× the rate of preventable medical errors, largely due to communication gaps. SignBridge's Healthcare Mode preloads medical vocabulary, simplifies jargon with Gemini before signing it, and keeps a written transcript for the patient to take home.
