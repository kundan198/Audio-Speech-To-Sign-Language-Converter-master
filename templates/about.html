About SignBridge

Conversation is a human right.

Around the world, 70 million people use a sign language as their first language. Yet most digital tools still assume everyone speaks. SignBridge exists to close that gap with real-time, two-way translation.

What it does

SignBridge translates both directions in real time. Speech becomes sign animations. Webcam hand gestures become spoken English. Live Conversation mode runs both sides together so Deaf and hearing users can talk naturally on one screen.

How it works

Speech to sign uses NLP preprocessing and sign-video sequencing. Sign to speech uses MediaPipe Hands for landmarks and Gemini Vision for recognition, then speaks with browser TTS or ElevenLabs. The system is built for low latency and practical conversations.

Why Healthcare Mode

Healthcare Mode simplifies doctor speech into plain language before signing, then keeps a transcript for follow-up clarity. This directly addresses communication gaps that increase preventable clinical risk.
